HOW TO APPLY THE BLACK MASK

  • Cleanse your face: Start with a clean, dry face. Use a gentle cleanser to remove any makeup, dirt, and oil from your skin. Pat dry with a clean towel.
  • Prepare the mask: Open the mask product and follow the instructions provided on the packaging.
  • Apply an even layer: Using a clean brush, spatula, or your fingertips, apply an even layer of the black mask to your face, avoid contact with eyes and lips. Apply a slightly thicker layer at the edges to make it easier to peel off later.
  • Peel off the mask: Once the mask is completely dry, gently start peeling it off from the edges. Start from the bottom and peel upward in an upward motion. Take care not to tug or pull too forcefully, especially if you have sensitive skin.
  • Rinse off residue: After peeling off the mask, rinse your face with lukewarm water to remove any residue. Use gentle, circular motions to ensure all the mask is washed off.

ALYA APPLICATION TIPS:
  • Preparation: Start with a clean, dry face. Wash your face using a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt, makeup, or impurities. Pat your skin dry with a clean towel.
  • Scoop and apply: Open the product and use the provided applicator or a clean brush/spatula to scoop out a small amount of the product and apply onto your face.
  • Apply and wait: Typically it takes about 10-15 minutes for the new alya skin product to dry but it's worth the wait. Letting the product set is a way of aiding it's efficiency.
  • Rinse off or remove: After the specified drying time, gently rinse off the mask using warm water. Massage your skin in circular motions while rinsing to provide a mild exfoliation effect. Alternatively, you can use a damp cloth or sponge to remove the mask.
